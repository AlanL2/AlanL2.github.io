About me

Hi, my name is Alan Li.


I am currently in grade 10 and in the MaCS program at William Lyon Mackenzie Collegiate Institute.


Things that I enjoy doing are reading, music, chess, video games, table tennis and badminton. I enjoy playing the saxophone in my spare time, while participating in my school jazz band and wind ensemble, and I am on the 2019-2020 Mackenzie Computer Programming Team Executive Team, where I enjoy collaborating with other people, setting up contests, and teaching lessons for younger grades.

I also participate a lot in different competitive programming contests, as this year I have become a lot more active in coding compared to the past few years. I take extra curricular classes to improve my skills in competitive coding, and participate in contests like the Lyon Computing Contest, Google Code Jam and the Canadian Computing Competition.


I currently have experience in:

  • Java
  • C++
  • Processing

In the future, I hope to be a computer scientist or a software engineer.
